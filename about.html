Our Process

This is how we work

We collect your clothes

Once you place your order, our team arranges a convenient pick-up time that suits your schedule. We collect your clothes directly from your home or office, saving you time and effort. Each order is carefully checked, sorted, and tagged to ensure nothing is misplaced. We also take note of any special instructions, such as delicate fabrics, leather items, or garments that require dry cleaning.

Wash your clothes

Your clothes are professionally cleaned using high-quality detergents and modern equipment suitable for each fabric type. We handle cloth, leather, and delicate garments with specialized care to maintain their quality and longevity. After washing or dry cleaning, every item is properly ironed and neatly folded, ensuring your clothes look fresh, clean, and ready to wear.

Get delivery

Once your laundry is complete, we package it carefully and arrange a prompt drop-off at your preferred location. Your clothes are returned clean, fresh, ironed, and folded, just the way you expect them. With Laundrius, you enjoy professional laundry care without leaving your home.

Call us for a service

We believe in clear communication and excellent customer service, so when you call Laundrius, you can expect prompt support, professional advice, and reliable service from start to finish.
